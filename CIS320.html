CIS 320: Systems Analysis and Design

This class introduced us to the system development life cycle. Our group Zu-ming (we didn't have a say in the name) worked on creating the cobWeb a website where CIS students could help mentor each other, find jobs, and receive important annoucements from adminstrators. In this class we went through the planning, analysis and design phases of the SDLC. In a future class I will be taking we will finsih a CIS 320's project by actually implementing it, the last phase of the SDLC. We mainly used UML as our methodoloy to complete this project.
